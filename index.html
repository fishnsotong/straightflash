Welcome to office delivery

Over here, you are able to send in your request for delivery within the office. This service is for your convinence to save your time by having the robot delivery food items to your deask.

How is it done?

You would need to specify your desired snacks and location for deliverying. With a small 5% service charge, the smart delivery robot will reach you in no time.
