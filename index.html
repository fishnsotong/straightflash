Welcome to office delivery

Over here, you are able to send in your request for delivery within the office. This service is for your convinence to save your time to either delivery to you food items or to help you to delivery a documnt (less then 500g) to another colleague.

How is it done?

You would need to specify your desired service and location for deliverying. With a click of $2 payment, the smart delivery robot will reach you in no time.
